Where the references come from.

Steal specifics, not the whole site.

apple.com/iphone
Tile-grid rhythm
Steal the 80ms staggered tile reveal and the 22px tile radius. Adapt: Mongolian-context product photography instead of US-market lifestyle.
apple.com/shop/buy-iphone
Sticky-rail configurator
Steal the right-rail summary that fades in 300ms after layout settles. Adapt: ₮ pricing, fleet-volume options, MNT 0% APR.
apple.com/macbook-pro
Marketing PDP cadence
Steal the slow scroll storytelling, one beat per viewport. Adapt: fleet-procurement narrative, not consumer aspiration.
combine-theme-gadget.myshopify.com
Single-typeface discipline
Steal: DM Sans throughout, 175ms linear motion default. Adapt: keep DM Sans for Latin, add Golos Text fallback for Cyrillic so Mongolian content doesn't fall back.
bang-olufsen.com
Photography weight
Steal: large, museum-quality, single-product hero shots. Adapt: photograph the actual MacBook Pro on Mongolian executive desks, not US lifestyle scenes.
aesop.com
Editorial copy density
Steal: long-form descriptions in narrow measure. Adapt: replace product romance with procurement-manager pragmatism (specs, warranty, MDM).
